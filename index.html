About Olute FC

Founded in 2023, Olute Football Club has grown to become one of the most respected grassroots football organizations in the region. We focus on:

  • Youth development programs
  • Professional coaching
  • Community engagement
  • Competitive tournaments

Our mission is to provide opportunities for young athletes to develop their skills, discipline, and sportsmanship.
