Olute Football Association - Terms and Conditions

1. Membership and Registration

All participants must complete a registration form truthfully and accurately. Parental/guardian consent is required for participants under 18 years old. Olute FA reserves the right to accept or reject any application without obligation to provide a reason.

2. Code of Conduct

Respect all players, staff, and officials. No abusive behavior or unsportsmanlike conduct will be tolerated. Players must attend sessions on time and in proper kit.

3. Fees and Payments

All fees must be paid before participating. Fees are non-refundable unless specified. Late payments may result in exclusion from training or matches.

4. Health and Safety

Disclose any medical conditions before participating. Olute FA staff may administer first aid in emergencies. Participation is at your own risk.

5. Media and Photography

By registering, you consent to the use of photos/videos for promotional purposes. Opt out in writing if you do not wish to be included.

6. Liability and Insurance

Olute FA is not liable for injuries or personal loss during activities. Personal accident insurance is recommended.

7. Program Changes and Cancellations

We may reschedule or cancel sessions due to weather or other reasons. Participants will be notified in advance where possible.

8. Data Protection

We collect only necessary data, securely store it, and never share it without your consent except when required by law.

9. Disciplinary Action

Violating these terms may lead to suspension or dismissal from Olute FA programs. All disciplinary decisions are final.

10. Acceptance of Terms

By registering or participating, you agree to all terms listed above. If you do not agree, please do not register or participate.
